About BEST

Board of European Students of Technology

BEST is a non-profit, non-political, non-governmental organisation that has existed since 1989. It is made up of students from technical universities across Europe who work to improve communication, cooperation and the exchange of ideas among European students.

Global Impact 🌍

Today, BEST is present in more than
90 universities across 33 countries, with over

3,000 active members who volunteer to provide development opportunities for students.

BEST Skopje

The local BEST Group Skopje was founded to connect students from the technical faculties in Skopje with the international BEST community. Our group is made up of motivated students who work on organising a variety of activities and events.

> What do we offer?
  • * Educational courses and workshops
  • * International student exchange
  • * Networking with companies
  • * Leadership skills development
  • * Cultural and social activities
