ABOUT US

BEST, Board of European Students of Technology, is an international non-profit and non-political student organization, organized and run entirely by students. BEST connects students from over 80 technical universities in 30 countries in a network that is maintained through numerous joint projects, interests and friendships. Every year it organizes over 120 international courses, seminars and conferences in which students from different countries learn and exchange experiences and ideas. Our priority is to offer quality and to assist students in Europe, and to do everything we can combine the triangle "students - university - companies".


BEST - Skopje is a full member of the great European family BEST. Thus, students of Ss. "Cyril and Methodius" University in Skopje have the right to use the services of BEST, including the participation of free academic events organized by other local BEST groups. BEST Skopje is officially founded in 2002, and has been providing endless opportunities for students since then. Through numerous events, BEST Skopje manages to offer students complementary education, career support and educational involvement, and has organised many editions of EBEC (European BEST Engineering Competition), Job Fair, LEoE (Local Event on Education), as well as BEST Courses, specifically in the summer season. In fact, this year we will be organizing our 20th BEST Course, and all of our members are very excited to welcome you and give you the most incredible and unforgettable 9 days of your student life!

SKOPJE

Skopje is the capital and largest city of the Republic of N.Macedonia. It is the country's political, cultural, economic, and academic center. It was known in the Roman period under the name Scupi. The territory of Skopje has been inhabited since at least 4000 BC; remains of Neolithic settlements have been found within the old Kale Fortress that overlooks the modern city centre. There are two artificial lakes located just outside of the city. Lake Matka is the result of the construction of a dam in the Matka Canyon in the 1930s, and Lake Treska was dug for leisure purpose in 1978. Other interesting landmarks are the Stone Bridge, the Millennium Cross, the Old Railway Station and the Old Bazaar.
