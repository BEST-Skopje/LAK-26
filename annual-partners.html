ANNUAL PARTNERS

Komercijalna Banka AD Skopje is a bank with a rich tradition, as well as a key partner of companies and a pillar of confidence for the citizens of our country. Accountability, transparency, efficiency, innovation and a strong domestic and international reputation are the values that make Komercijalna Banka traditionally the client's first choice.

As a universal bank, it offers a wide range of banking services — from collecting deposits, providing loans to legal entities and individuals, issuing bank cards, providing international and domestic payment operations services, offering digital and mobile banking, mediation in purchases of foreign currency, securities, forfeiting, and more.

Together with Komercijalna Banka, most companies in the country can rely on a steady road to success. Through the years of its development, the Bank has managed to create new values, open connections to the world, and build a highly professional team of experts. Through its widespread network of branches across the country, the Bank offers a wide range of traditional and digital products and services to individuals and companies. For fast and efficient international services, the Bank has established correspondent banking relations with 465 banks in 70 countries.

The Bank also focuses on community values. Through continuous financial support of culture, sports, and society in general, Komercijalna Banka creates a better world and a healthier and cleaner living environment for all.

Infinum is a digital product agency founded in 2005 by two engineering students who won an app-building contest — before the App Store even existed — and used the prize money to start a company.

Today, Infinum partners with forward-thinking brands, organisations, and founders that understand the value of technology. Together, they challenge how things are done, reimagine business models, and introduce innovative solutions to the market. The agency offers expertise across product strategy and design, mobile and web apps, AI and data engineering, custom IoT solutions, enterprise platforms, cybersecurity, and brand experience.

Behind the screens of every delivered solution is a team that asked the questions others didn't think to ask. Infinum currently counts nearly 400 people across 22 teams and has earned over 200 pounds' worth of design and business awards, including Red Dot, iF Design, Awwwards, and Deloitte Fast 50.

Infinum also believes in giving back: they publish open-source tools, run the Infinum Academy to teach design and development, host community events, and support social initiatives through the Infinum Foundation.
